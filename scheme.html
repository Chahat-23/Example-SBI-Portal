SOCIETY SCHEMES





Society Scheme for Members



At the time of retirement of the members who have completed minimum ten years of membership are entitled to get an honorarium of Rs.10,000/- under Retirement Benefit Scheme of the society.



Guest House



The Society has its own guest house which is provided to its members at nominal charges. It is situated at a very prime location of Bhopal at E-5/13 A, Arera Colony.( Phone No 0755-2553972).
The charges are as under:

  1. One Bed Rs.100/- per day

  2. One Room Rs.300/- per day

  3. Complete Guest House Rs.3000/- perday for Member and Rs5000/- for others

(One Hall,Kitchen,Dining room, 2 Bed room) Note : Rs.2000/- will be deposited as security with the society at the time of guest house booking and other charges like water,electricity,sweeper etc. will be adjusted in this amount.



Scholarship



Society provides as under, scholarship to the wards of its members to encourage in their studies :- Students who pass 8th standard exam with 80% and above marks are entitled to get Rs.1500/- (one time) Students who pass 10th standard exam with 80% and above marks are entitled to get Rs.2000/- (one time Students who pass 12th standard exam with 80% and above marks are entitled to get Rs.2500/- ( one time ) W.E.F academic year 2009-10. Students who pass higher exam (graduation/post graduation) with 70% and above marks are entitled to get Rs.2500/- ( one time ) W.E.F academic year 2009-10
